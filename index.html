Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock

Here is how to play the game:

Below are five choices: Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock

  1. Rock Beats Scissors & Lizard.
  2. Scissors beats Paper & Lizard.
  3. Paper beats Rock & Spock.
  4. Lizard beats Paper & Spock.
  5. Spock beats Scissor & Rock.

Each winner receives a point. The first to ten points wins a round!

Three rounds wins the game.


Good luck, and enjoy!
